## Research Contributions

### Meta-Analysis Results
- **52 empirical studies** (2019-2025)
- **Overall effect:** Hedges' g = 0.783 (95% CI: 0.719-0.847)
- **Heterogeneity:** I² = 32.8% (moderate)
- **Industrial systems:** g = 0.915 (26% improvement over educational kits)

### Technology Taxonomy
- **Level 1-2:** Educational kits (d = 0.59-0.64)
- **Level 3:** Advanced educational (d = 0.68)
- **Level 4:** Didactic industrial (d = 0.73)
- **Level 5:** Industrial-grade (d = 0.94)

### Cost-Effectiveness
- **Remote laboratories:** $45/student (optimal)
- **Physical industrial labs:** $280/student
- **Impact per $1000:** Remote labs 5.8× superior
- **Educational kits:** $28-45/student (lowest cost, lower effectiveness)

### HRC Validation
- **Platform:** Fanuc M-20iA industrial manipulator
- **Episodes:** 1000 training iterations
- **Metrics:** Throughput, workload, safety
- **Results:** Successful multi-objective optimization
